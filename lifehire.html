LifeHire Your Life. Live it.

The mission of LifeHire is to change the way people live and work. LifeHire is a community where those needing work done and those looking for work can find each other.

Work

Can you teach the clarinet, build a fence, mow a yard, clean a house or fix things? It's time to put your skills to work for you. Whether you want to work full time, or just make some extra cash, LifeHire lets you bid on the jobs that interest you and fit your schedule. On Lifehire you see jobs available that require the skills you possess, and can submit a bid for whatever you choose.

Hire

Do you want to learn the clarinet? Need a fence built, your lawn mowed, your house cleaned, or something fixed? If your home is like ours, there always seems to be a list of things that need to be done. Find the help you need, when you need it, from a member of your community. You can even choose to support specific groups in your community such as firefighters, teachers, or students.

How LifeHire Works

LifeHire is very simple to use. When you need a job done you can quickly and easily post the job on a LifeHire "board". If you are looking for work, you can subscribe to the boards that interest you and you'll be instantly notified when another LifeHire user posts a job. Then you can hop on and bid on available jobs. There is no cost for posting or bidding on jobs. Once the work is done LifeHire will automatically handle the payment processing for you!
